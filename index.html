About Sweet Haven

Three generations of baking excellence

Founded in 1995 by master baker Maria Santos, Sweet Haven has been serving the community with authentic, handcrafted desserts for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional techniques, and innovative flavors has made us a beloved local institution.

Today, Maria's daughter Sofia continues the family tradition, blending time-honored recipes with modern presentation to create desserts that delight both the eye and palate.
